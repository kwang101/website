SpaceX Launch

Rocket Launch: NET March 2019
SpaceX Falcon Heavy Arabsat 6A

As an ex-Tesla Software Engineer Intern, I'm always interested in news regarding the brother/sister companies that Elon Musk is managing. A SpaceX Falcon Heavy rocket will launch the Arabsat 6A communications satellite, built by Lockheed Martin, from Kennedy Space Center. The satellite will deliver television, internet and mobile phone services to the Middle East, Africa and Europe. Arabsat-6A is part of the two-satellite Arabsat-6G program for Arabsat.
